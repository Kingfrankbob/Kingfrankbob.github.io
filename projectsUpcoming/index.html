Advent of Code:

Every year I do the Advent of Code, it is a 25 day challenge where you have to solve a puzzle.
The puzzles get harder and harder as the days go on. I have done it every year since 2021, and I
have only missed but a few days. I have done it with a few friends, and I have done it alone. It is a fun
challenge that I look forward to every year. I have done it in C#, and once in C++.
